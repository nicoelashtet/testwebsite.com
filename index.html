Research Interest

My research interests include big data analytics, software engineering, cloud computing, natural language processing and information retrieval. My current research relates to three specific areas:
  • i) Graph Database,
  • ii) Smart Campus application: information retrieval of student performance data by face recognition
  • iii) Data analysis and data visualization on global education data, global migration data
